Ethical decision-making dilemma and governance of autonomous driving

2019-12-09 1 min read # political science

The rapid development of driverless technology has forced humans to face up to the ethical decision-making dilemmas they may encounter and to think about whether an algorithm needs to be pre-set to help them make ethical decisions. Starting from the fundamental problem of the necessity of setting ethical decision-making algorithms, this paper focuses on the ethical basis of setting ethical decision-making algorithms, the decision-making power of ethical decision-making algorithms and the solution of ownership rights. Finally, suggestions are made from the perspective of governance.
